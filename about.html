Explorations

What is graphic design to me? It is about giving shapes an intention, a purpose.

I discovered this at a young age when I was playing my racing videos games. Even though driving around a race track in my dream cars was fun, I was drawn more to the customization section. Graphics are an aspect of a cars appearance and to be able to build designs in those games they only provided basic rectangles and circles to create from.

After spending countless hours letting my imagination run wild in those games, I fell in love with how the basic shapes could be tweaked and placed together to represent a meaning or create a feeling.

From this moment onwards I grew a strong interest in understanding shapes, typography, and logos for communicating in a visual way.
